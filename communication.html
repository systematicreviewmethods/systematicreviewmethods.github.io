Communication


This session will outline how systematic reviewers may communicate the results of their reviews, including how to engage with stakeholders, and how to produce tailored media to describe results.


Learning objectives:

  • To appreciate that systematic review and map reports are long, technical reports that can be difficult to digest
  • To be aware that communication is a vital step with systematic reviews and maps
  • To be aware of some common methods of summarising reviews
  • To appreciate the importance of stakeholder engagement during communication
